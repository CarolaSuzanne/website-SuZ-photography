About SüZ

The world seems organized, but when you look again, there are so many things we can not explain. What seems normal can become absurd, when we can't fit it in our understanding of what we were taught. I like to look again and make work that is alienating but still from our world. Objects can have multiple purposes and often you can use or see them in different ways. Or even things we judge as ‘normal’ can be turned into other forms, another image. What is reality?


My name is Carola Suzanne. I live in Amsterdam and I also lived in Berlin for two years. I graduated from the Gerrit Rietveld Academie in Amsterdam and I studied Clinical Psychology at the University of Amsterdam.
